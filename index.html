Our most frequently asked questions.

how do i install the panels?

Our panels are easy to install. If you want a class A sound absorption, we recommend installing the panels with battens. If you're going to use them for decorative purposes only, we recommend screwing the panels directly into the wall through the acoustic felt backing using colour screws.

Where can the panels be installed?

The Acupanel panels are incredibly versatile. They can be installed on walls and ceilings, in living rooms, bedrooms, home offices, and commercial gyms-the list is endless! They can also be installed in kitchens andbathrooms, as long as they are not in direct contact with water. We've even seen our customers use the panels in campervan renovations.

How do I cut the panels?

The panels are simple to cut to size! You can use a sharp,fine-tooth saw to cut the panels. We recommend sanding down the cut edges of the panelfor a smooth edge finish. Did you know we offer a panel cutting service? Give our Customer Care team a call today to find out more.

Do you offer custom panel sizes?

Our panels are available in a range of sizes to cater to most projects. We also offer a custom size cutting service. Speak to our Customer Care team today for more information on our cutting service.
